Three types of Attachments exist. 

- Images
- Audio
- Video

In case of Images, we simply paste them.

In case of Audios, we will use online hosting services. 
I am using Internet Archive and Soundgasm.

For Videos, we will be using Youtube.

Audios can be made into videos and then can be uploaded from Youtube. 

As of February 2025, you are also using Self-hosting services so you can use them too. 


| Type of Media | What we are doing with it                        | Secondary |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | --------- |
| Images        | Paste them in Obsidian vault, renamed via plugin | -         |
| Audios        | Internet Archive Only                            | Soundgasm |
| Videos        | Youtube for videos                               | Substack  |
